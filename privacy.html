Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Welcome to Physics Lover 2.0. We are committed to maintaining your trust and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how our platform operates regarding your data and personal information.

1. Zero Server-Side Data Collection

Physics Lover 2.0 is designed to strictly respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any Personal Data (such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location) to our own servers or databases. The core functionality of our application works independently without requiring you to create an account, log in, or provide personal information to us.

2. Local Storage (On-Device Data)

To provide a personalized and seamless experience, our application uses your device's built-in Local Storage. This is used solely to save your preferences directly on your device, which includes:

  • Saved or bookmarked notes and chapters for quick access.
  • Recently opened history and study planner targets.
  • Theme preferences (e.g., Dark Mode or Light Mode).

This data remains strictly isolated inside your own device's browser or app storage. It is never uploaded to our servers, nor is it shared with any third party.

3. Third-Party Services Integration

To provide you with expert doubt-solving support and high-quality content, our application integrates certain trusted third-party services:

  • Tawk.to (Ask Doubt): If you use the live chat widget, you may be prompted to enter basic details (Name/Email) by Tawk.to. This information is processed securely by Tawk.to's servers. We do not intercept or store this data on our servers.
  • YouTube & Google Drive: Video lectures and PDF notes are hosted on YouTube and Google Drive. When interacting with these links, their respective Privacy Policies and Terms of Service apply.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Physics Lover 2.0 does not employ invasive tracking cookies, web beacons, or hidden background analytics tools to monitor your behavior. Your study sessions, clicks, and reading habits remain entirely private to you.

5. Children's Privacy

As an educational platform tailored for students from Class 7 to 12, our core application does not actively collect any personal data, making it completely safe for students of all ages. Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's use of third-party features like the "Ask Doubt" live chat.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as our platform evolves or legal requirements change. Any modifications will be posted on this page along with an updated "Effective Date". We encourage you to review this page periodically.
