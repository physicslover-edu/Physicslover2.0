Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Welcome to Physics Lover 2.0. We are committed to maintaining your trust and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how our Application operates regarding your data.

1. Zero Server-Side Data Collection

Physics Lover 2.0 is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any Personal Data (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location) to our own servers or databases. The core functionality of our Application works independently without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information to us.

2. Local Storage (On-Device Data)

To enhance your experience, our Application uses your device's built-in Local Storage. This is used solely to save your personal preferences directly on your device, including:

  • Saved or bookmarked chapters for quick access.
  • Theme preferences (e.g., Dark Mode or Light Mode).
  • Language settings (English/Bengali).

This data remains strictly inside your own device's browser/app storage. It is never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third party by us.

3. Third-Party Services: "Ask Doubt" Feature

To provide you with expert doubt-solving support, our Application integrates a third-party live chat widget provided by Tawk.to.

  • Data Submission: When you initiate a conversation through the "Ask Doubt" feature, you may be prompted to enter basic details such as your Name, Email Address, and Class.
  • Data Handling: Please be aware that this information is submitted directly to the Tawk.to secure servers to facilitate the chat session. We do not host, intercept, or store this data within the Physics Lover 2.0 application or our personal servers.
  • Third-Party Policy: Your interactions within the chat widget are governed by Tawk.to's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Physics Lover 2.0 does not employ invasive tracking cookies, web beacons, or background analytics tools to monitor your behavior. Your study sessions remain entirely private.

5. Children's Privacy

Our core Application does not actively collect any personal data, making it safe for students of all ages. However, if a user under the age of 13 wishes to use the "Ask Doubt" (Tawk.to) feature, we recommend doing so under parental guidance, as the third-party chat service may request a name and email to initiate the session.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as our Application evolves. Any modifications will be posted on this page along with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
