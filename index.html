About Physics Lover 2.0

Physics Lover 2.0 is a strictly non-profit, independent digital learning ecosystem carefully structured to simplify the intricacies of Physics and generalized science curriculum for students encompassing Grade 7 through Grade 12.

Founded and maintained by educator Subhajit Biswas, the foundational objective of this platform is to bridge the gap between complex theoretical physics abstractions and core mathematical applications. We offer comprehensive academic notes, meticulously step-by-step solved question banks, structural board exam guidelines, and high-yield conceptual video lessons absolutely free of any expense.

Our long-term academic vision is anchored in the absolute democratization of scientific education, making high-quality study resources universally accessible, streamlined, and friction-free for every student across the region.

PRIVACY POLICY & TERMS

Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection and Use

Physics Lover 2.0 operates strictly as an open-access educational platform. We do not require account registration, nor do we collect, process, or store any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, phone numbers, or email addresses. Your usage remains entirely anonymous.

2. Local Data Storage & Cookies

To improve your experience, this application uses standard browser Local Storage to save UI preferences, such as your bookmarked classes or last visited pages. This data is strictly confined to your personal device's hardware and is never transmitted to any external server or third-party database. We do not use tracking cookies.

3. Educational Fair Use Declaration

All academic solutions, references, and curricular materials presented on this platform are structured solely for private study, non-commercial research, and educational advancement. This constitutes "Fair Dealing" under Section 52 of the Indian Copyright Act, 1957. The platform is strictly non-profit and contains no commercial monetization schemes.

4. Disclaimer of Liability

While maximum care has been taken to ensure mathematical and scientific accuracy within our solutions and built-in computational engines, the developer disclaims all liability for any incidental typing errors, calculation anomalies, or discrepancies. Students are advised to use this service as a supplemental tool alongside their official school curriculum.
